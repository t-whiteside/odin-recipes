Oatmeal

Description

Nothing satisfys the soul on a chilly morning than a delicious bowl of oatmeal. Enjoy this dish with your favorite fruit on top.

Ingredients

  • Oatmeal
  • Brown sugar
  • Maple syrup
  • Butter
  • Fruit of choice

Steps

  1. Cook oatmeal to box/bag directions
  2. Add in brown sugar, maple syrup, and butter
  3. Top with fruit
