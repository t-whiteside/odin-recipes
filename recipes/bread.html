Bread

Description

Featured Artisan Bread from the Oklahoma bakery, Daily Bread Co. This recipe will show you how to make the delicious dough offered by the young baker.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Flour
  • 1 1/2 cups Water
  • 1/2 teaspoon Yeast
  • 1 teaspoon Salt

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients together
  2. Form into a ball
  3. Wrap bowl with saran wrap
  4. Let dough sit for 12-18 hours
  5. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.
  6. Bake Dutch oven with lid for 30 mins
  7. Bake bread for 30 mins with lid
  8. Bake bread for 15 mins without lid
  9. Let cool and enjoy
